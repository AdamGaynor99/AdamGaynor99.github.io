Overnight Oats

Simple, but delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • 50g of rolled porridge oats
  • 2 tablespoons of natural yogurt
  • 50g mixed berries
  • Drizzle of honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon of peanut butter

Method

  1. The night before serving, stir the cinnamon and 100ml of water into your oats with a pinch of salt.
  2. The next day, loosen with a little more water if needed. Top with the yogurt, berries, a drizzle of honey and the peanut butter.
